Konversation Karlsruhe

I worked with Volksbank Karlsruhe, a German bank. They wanted to launch a campaign to attract new customers between 18 and 35 years old. With my team, we thought about a solution to attract this young audience. Naturally, we focused our idea on the digital area and more especially a web or a mobile application.

So, our idea was to create a community of young people to help each other on troubles that they can encounter, to give you a simple idea it is like airbnb's help section. However we wanted something different than just a "forum" so we decided to focus on accessibility and the easiness to use by developping a web and mobile application
