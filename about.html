About

Graphic design enthusiast, I travel the world and the web, in order to find the latest fad in web design. I put my skills in practice to create content that inspires me. I'm excited to see what will come in the future of this digital era and I hope to contribute to improving it

View My Works

I was born in France, from an Italian father and a Chinese mother, this culture clash influenced a lot my tastes for culture and traditions. Growing as a kid I was already super curious and ambitious, I was always aiming for bigger. After high school, I decided to start traveling. I discovered the joy of acquiring knowledge by traveling. My First trip was to China, a lovely culture and a very active and permanently changing country.

I then had the opportunity to travel while studying, I discovered Greece, a country that I fell in love with. I also did an Erasmus exchange in Ireland, a very enriching experience, and such a different culture from a country so close to France. I also did a road trip to Italy by motorcycle. And more recently I did an interrail trip to East-Europe.

That being said, I am certain that observing different cultures is a must for designers because "design" is impracticable without understanding human behavior. This is the basis of good design, it's always starting from an interaction within humans, and so it is necessary to understand human nature.I am currently studying interface design at Paris 13 university. I intend to work in the digital area as a designer, At the moment I am an intern in a digital agency in Paris

目标

Target

Goals

Goals are meant to be achieved, that's why I like to set some realistic goals. Having a career plan is very interesting as it allows you to put you in good tracks to see the future.

Goals

1 - TRAVEL

Traveling is not only a hobby, but it is also necessary for me. I hate routine and traveling allow me to escape from routine. Moreover, thanks to my trips I acquire some experience that I can only get by traveling. Traveling helps you to see outside the box, a required vision when you are a designer. After years, you take a different approach to solving problems when you have this knowledge.

2 - REINFORCE SKILLS

There are different ways to get experience, some people can be very regular and repeat the same action over and over and they will for sure with time get better at something. That is something I have to work on. Improving my consistency in learning something. Breaking the procrastination to do better stuff is my principal concern.

3 - BUILD A NETWORK

Among all these years and trips, I have come to meet a lot of people, I have stayed in touch with a few of them. Years after years, watching everyone evolve is very exciting. Having the right person to help you or to help at the right moment is super important for your career.

4 - START A NEW PROJECT

I want to create something, something big. But I have first to start slowly to then maybe encounter success. It requires a lot of courage to start something and take a risk of launching a project. Knowing that 80% of projects fail, it is scary at first. I might make the first step soon.
