User need vs user satisfaction

Date: 24/12/18

Author: Clément CARMINATI

When designing an app, you always care about the user being satisfied, you want him to like what you have designed. Sometimes this feeling can be so strong that you don't even satisfy the user needs as long as he is happy.
The same way as a dumb client request, you know it's stupid and bad but you will still do it because he makes him happy.

Nowadays people are not opened enough to accept change or evolution because it takes an effort for the user. If we want to build something better we have to make efforts, changing our routine and our bad habits.

We are living in a society where anything is accessible very easily, we consume a lot compared to the last century. And this way of consumption became natural, we just get used to it and unfortunately, this is bad for innovation. We are just highering our standards of comfort and thinking about traveling back in the past sounds impossible.

I'm not saying that humans became lazier it's just that if we keep going straight without looking back we'll just end up in the wall. Let go off the break and close the eyes isn't a viable solution.

As designers, we always have to remember how stuff is done and I think that if users understands how it is made too, it will allow them to change their vision on the world and on their needs.
